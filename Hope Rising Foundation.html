Our Mission


At Hope Rising Foundation, we are dedicated to empowering incarcerated children and supporting their families. Our mission is to provide comprehensive support services, advocate for their rights, and promote positive outcomes for children affected by parental incarceration.



Our Programs


Family Visitation Support


We offer assistance for families to maintain connections through visitation programs and support services, ensuring that children maintain strong bonds with their incarcerated parents.



Educational Enrichment


Our educational programs provide incarcerated children with access to tutoring, mentoring, and educational resources to support their academic success and future opportunities.



Emotional Support Services


We offer counseling, therapy, and other emotional support services to help children cope with the challenges of parental incarceration and build resilience for the future.
